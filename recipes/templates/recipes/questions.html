How do you create a new recipe?

Make sure you are logged in or have registered and signed in order to create a recipe. Head over to create recipe section in the navigation bar, fill in the title, discription, method and ingredients (no need for the other content if not known). Click the add recipe button and the recipe should appear on the home page and for easier access it will be located in your profile section.

How do you edit/update an existing recipe?

Make sure you are logged in or have registered and signed in order to edit a recipe. Head over to your recipes, click the update button in the recipe detail. Here you can edit the title, discription, method, ingredients and all other content. Once you are happy with your updates, click the update button and the recipe will update live. You can only edit and update your own created recipes.

How do you delete a recipe?

Make sure you are logged in or have registered and signed in order to delete a recipe. Head over to your recipe detail and click the delete button next to the recipe. You can only delete your own recipes.

How do you like or comment on a recipe?

Make sure you are logged in or have registered and signed in order to like or comment on a recipe. In any recipe detail you are able to like and unlike any recipe, comment on a recipe. Explicit comment will not be tolerated and your accout will be deleted on inspection
